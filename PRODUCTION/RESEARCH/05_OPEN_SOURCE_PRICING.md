### **License Selection**

#### **Core Wallet: MIT License**
**Why MIT?**
- ‚úÖ Most permissive (encourages adoption)
- ‚úÖ Can be used in proprietary software
- ‚úÖ Trusted by developers
- ‚úÖ Used by React, Node.js, jQuery

**Alternatives Considered:**
- ‚ùå GPL: Too restrictive, limits adoption
- ‚ùå Apache 2.0: Patent clause may scare some
- ‚ùå BSL: Confusing, limits commercial use

#### **Premium Features: Proprietary**
**Why Proprietary?**
- ‚úÖ Protect competitive advantage
- ‚úÖ Enable revenue generation
- ‚úÖ Can open source later if needed
